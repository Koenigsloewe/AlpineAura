AlpineAura Return Policy

At AlpineAura, we are committed to providing our customers with high-quality hiking and outdoor gear. We understand that sometimes a product may not fit your needs or expectations. In these cases, we offer a straightforward and customer-friendly return policy.

Return Period

You have 30 days from the date of purchase to initiate a return.

Condition of Returned Items

  • Items must be returned in their original condition, unworn, with tags attached, and in their original packaging.
  • Items that are damaged, altered, or show signs of wear may not be eligible for a return or exchange.

How to Initiate a Return

  • 1. Visit our online Returns Center and fill out the return request form.
  • 2. Include a brief description of the reason for the return.
  • 3. You will receive a return authorization number and a prepaid shipping label via email.

Return Shipping

We offer free return shipping for items within the continental United States. For international returns, customers are responsible for return shipping costs.

Refunds

Refunds will be processed to the original method of payment within 5-7 business days of receiving the returned item. Note: Shipping charges are non-refundable.

Exchanges

If you wish to exchange an item, please follow the return procedure and place a new order for the item you desire.

Defective or Incorrect Items

If you receive a defective or incorrect item, please contact us immediately at support@alpineaura.com. We will expedite a replacement and cover all associated shipping costs.

Additional Information

We reserve the right to limit or decline returns or exchanges that do not comply with our policy. This policy is subject to change without notice.

Contact Us

For any questions regarding our return policy or for assistance with your return, please contact our customer service team at support@alpineaura.com. We're here to help!
